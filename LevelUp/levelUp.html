Why Level Up? 🧙

Transform your everyday habits into epic quests! “Level Up” is an RPG-style habit tracker where brushing teeth gives EXP and drinking water makes you stronger. Simple tasks become epic as you gain gold, level up, and unlock skills.

Each habit completed grants EXP — grow stronger daily.

💼

Inventory

Unlock weapons, potions & gear as you complete tasks.

🔥

Skill Tree

Daily Quests

Do quests (habits) every day. Stay on your streak!

Hey adventurer! 🖖 I built Level Up to bring joy, discipline, and a bit of fantasy into everyday life. Why should only games be fun and rewarding? Let’s turn life into an RPG.

Everything is built using native Android tools and sprinkled with some ✨ magic spells ✨ from Solo Leveling. Build discipline. Earn gold. Unlock skills. Become the main character!
